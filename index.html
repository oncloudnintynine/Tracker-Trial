System Architecture

  • Air Bounds: Hardcoded to Singapore region to prevent 429 Too Many Requests from OpenSky.
  • Security: All remote connections utilize WSS or HTTPS to comply with strict browser mixed-content policies.
  • Cleanup: Stale markers are automatically garbage-collected (60s for air, 5m for sea).
